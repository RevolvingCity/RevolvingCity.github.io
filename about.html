About Us

CONTEXT

BRIEF HISTORY OF REVOLVINGCITY

Founded in 2020, Revolving City is a social enterprise dedicated to transforming and democratizing education in Africa and also making global opportunities accessible, particularly in Africa. Motivated by the dire need to address the gap in quality education and the high number of out-of-school children, we pioneered a revolutionary approach to learning. Our initiative breaks away from traditional methods by introducing digital education to remote areas, thus democratizing access to knowledge. By harnessing the power of technology and a team of skilled volunteer teachers, we have made significant strides in bringing high-quality, accessible education to underprivileged children. Our journey, driven by a passion for change and social responsibility, underscores our commitment to bridging the educational divide and empowering the next generation with global opportunities. However, we have evolved over the years , Revolving City has emerged as a beacon of hope, aiming to tackle the challenges faced by Africans in accessing global educational and career opportunities. This was motivated by a commitment to simplify complex processes such as applying to international institutions, securing scholarships, and navigating visa requirements. We recognized the unique hurdles faced by the African community and responded by offering tailored guidance and support, including personalized advice and counseling. Our mission has always been clear: to transform the aspirations of Africans into tangible achievements, making the pursuit of a global education or career not just a dream, but a reachable goal. This dedication underpins our journey, driving us to be more than a service provider, but a trusted ally in realizing the global ambitions of our clients.

CORE VALUES

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Navigating Complex Global Opportunities

We simplify the daunting process of applying to international institutions, securing scholarships, and understanding visa requirements, turning these complex tasks into manageable steps tailored for Africans aspiring to study or work abroad.

Tailored Guidance and Support

Our services are specifically designed to address the unique challenges faced by Africans in the global educational and career landscape, providing personalized career guidance, language training, and legal advice to ensure you're well-prepared for success.

Transforming Aspirations into Achievement

We are more than just a service provider; we are your ally in realizing your global ambitions. Our comprehensive approach ensures that your journey towards a global education or career is not just a dream, but an attainable reality, equipped with the right tools and knowledge.

STATEMENT

OUR VISION

To be the leading catalyst in reshaping the educational and professional landscape of Africa. Our vision is to create a world where every African has unimpeded access to global educational and career opportunities, fostering a generation of leaders, innovators, and change-makers who will drive progress and development both on the continent and in the global arena. We envision a future where the potential of African talent is recognized and nurtured, contributing significantly to the global community through education, innovation, and leadership.

STATEMENT

OUR MISSION

  • Expand Digital Education Reach: Enhance and broaden the scope of digital education initiatives to make quality education accessible in more remote and underserved areas across Africa.
  • Strengthen Global Partnerships: Collaborate with international educational institutions and organizations to create more opportunities for scholarships, exchanges, and cultural programs.
  • Cultivate Local Talent: Invest in local talent development through workshops, mentorship programs, and entrepreneurship support, focusing on nurturing leadership, innovation, and problem-solving skills.
  • Advocate for Educational Policy Reform: Engage with policymakers to advocate for reforms that support and prioritize access to global education and career opportunities for Africans.
  • Build a Community Network: Develop a strong, supportive network of alumni, professionals, and like-minded organizations to foster mentorship, collaboration, and shared learning, creating a sustainable ecosystem for continuous growth and opportunity sharing.
